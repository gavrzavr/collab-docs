Q1 2026 Business Review

Summary of Q1 2026 results and Q2 plans. Written and edited live together with the team, via the PostPaper editor.

Summary

Strong quarter overall — revenue up 18%, churn down to 2.1%, and we shipped 14 product features including the long-awaited dashboard redesign.

  • Revenue reached $2.4M (+18% QoQ).
  • 14 new features shipped, of which 5 were on the Q1 roadmap.
  • Two enterprise contracts signed in January.

Revenue

Revenue reached $2.4M, up 18% from Q4 2025. Growth came primarily from enterprise contracts signed in January.

Enterprise contracts

Two large accounts were closed during the quarter, adding $400K in ARR. Both deals include a 3-year commitment and premium SLAs.

SMB segment

SMB revenue was stable at $600K. Self-serve conversion improved by 12% after the onboarding redesign in February.

Churn

Net churn dropped to 2.1% — our lowest rate ever. The customer success team's quarterly-review program is the most likely driver.

Product shipped

We shipped 14 user-facing features this quarter. Highlights below.

New dashboard

Full rewrite of the analytics dashboard — faster load, cleaner typography, and a new "at-a-glance" widget customers have been requesting since launch.

Collaboration improvements

Live cursors, presence avatars, and block-level comments landed in March. Early feedback from power users is overwhelmingly positive.

Hiring & team

Three new engineers joined in Q1. Engineering headcount is now 12. We also opened a senior design role that we expect to close in Q2.

Next quarter plan

Focus areas for Q2:

  • Launch the AI co-author feature currently in beta.
  • Roll out SSO for enterprise customers.
  • Expand the European data-residency offering.
